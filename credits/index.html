 ABOUT 

Presented by Spring Fest, Labyrinth 11.0 is an Online Mind Maze which can be quite confusing and frustrating. Answers can always be extracted by finding,following and linking all hidden clues and hints presented in your screen or webpage. Clues can be kept anywhere in the page like an image, URL etc. Using other possible senses apart from common-sense may be helpful. Remember! Hints can knock your door via anything possible under the sky. Ignoring any of them may lead to severe struggle.
